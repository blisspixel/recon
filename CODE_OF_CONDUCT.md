# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

recon is an open, professional project. Everyone who takes part, whether by
filing issues, opening pull requests, or joining discussions, helps keep it a
respectful and welcoming space for people of every background and level of
experience.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate.
- Assume good faith, and welcome newcomers and questions.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the people who use it.
- Give feedback kindly and accept it gracefully.

## Unacceptable behavior

Conduct that a reasonable person would consider hostile, demeaning, or
unprofessional has no place here, in project spaces or in public spaces when
representing the project.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report concerns privately to nick@pueo.io. Reports are handled confidentially
and in good faith. Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions, and
may warn or remove participants, for behavior they judge inappropriate.
