# Agentic UX validation: v1.9.2 historical aggregate

This is the aggregate decision record for the v1.9.2 bridge validation.
Detailed prompts, responses, provider metadata, target rows, and raw run
records are intentionally not tracked. New runs default to the gitignored
`validation/agentic_ux/local/` directory.

## Method

The historical matrix contained 12 sessions:

- 3 isolated personas: security analyst, due-diligence researcher, and
  operations engineer
- 2 synthetic fixture shapes: one evidence-dense and one deliberately sparse
- 2 modes per persona and fixture: fusion fields present and fusion fields
  removed

The prompts did not name posterior fields, sparse flags, uncertainty bands,
or the explanation DAG. The rubric measured whether each persona discovered
and used those surfaces without being directed to them.

## Aggregate results

- Posterior block read: 3 of 12 sessions
- Numeric uncertainty band cited: 1 of 12 sessions
- Explanation DAG suggested: 0 of 12 sessions
- Sparse-versus-dense behavior changed: 1 of 3 persona comparisons
- Fusion-on versus fusion-off behavior changed: 1 of 3 persona comparisons

## Decisions supported

1. Keep posterior observations because technical personas used them and the
   sparse case gained value from the numeric uncertainty band.
2. Do not assume narrative personas will translate posterior fields into
   hedged prose without a clearer user-facing summary.
3. Treat explanation-DAG discoverability as a product-surface issue. The
   JSON shape alone did not lead any persona to that escalation path.
4. Keep sparse evidence visible in rendered output because two of three
   persona comparisons did not change tone based on JSON fields alone.

## Privacy and reproducibility

This record was reduced to aggregate evidence on 2026-07-18. The historical
run was not repeated, and no new paid validation was performed. Current
fixtures use explicit synthetic sentinels under the reserved `.invalid`
namespace while preserving the tested dense and sparse structures.

To run the matrix locally, use:

`python -m validation.agentic_ux.run --output validation/agentic_ux/local/report.md`

Keep reports and optional raw records under `validation/agentic_ux/local/` or
`validation/agentic_ux/runs/`. Both paths are gitignored.
